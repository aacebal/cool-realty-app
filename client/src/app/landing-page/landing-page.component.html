About Rate My Realty

Rate My Realty allows users to rate realty listings to help improve the quality of pictures, descriptions and other details.

Users earn points and rewards for helping others create better listings, and gain relevance in the Rate My Realty community to be helped with their listing as well.

Connect with users from all over the web to share tips on how to advertise your property, and create a platform for a world of better listings.
